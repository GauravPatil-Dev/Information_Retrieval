Illinois Tech’s 2024 Summer College Programs for High School and Middle School Students

The one-of-a-kind Elevate College Prep allows middle school and high school students to take part in fun and rewarding summer college prep programs. These students will broaden their knowledge and enhance their skills through engaging programs that offer a variety of Chicago and suburban summer activities, allowing them to explore STEM fields, business, law, the arts, and more. They will gain valuable skills, earn college credit (in select programs), and get a taste of college life at Illinois Tech.

All students who complete their program will receive an official certificate of completion.

Learn more about the programming options available for summer 2024 below.

Application Deadline: May 3, 2024*

*Note: Many programs are offered on a first-come, first-served basis. Applying early is recommended, as seats may fill up before the deadline. Check individual program pages for specific deadline details.

To learn more about financial assistance information, please visit the Policies and Resources page.
